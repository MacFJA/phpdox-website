Symfony\Component\Form\FormTypeInterface::buildView

Builds the form view.

This method is called for each type in the hierarchy starting form the top most type. Type extensions can further modify the view. A view of a form is built before the views of the child forms are built. This means that you cannot access child views in this method. If you need to do so, move your logic to instead.
