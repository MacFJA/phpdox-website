Doctrine\ORM\QueryBuilder::getRootEntities

Gets the root entities of the query. This is the entity aliases involved in the construction of the query.

<code> $qb = $em->createQueryBuilder() ->select('u') ->from('User', 'u'); $qb->getRootEntities(); // array('User') </code>
